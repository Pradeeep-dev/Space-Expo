🏆 History

Key Space Milestones

1957
Sputnik 1

World's first artificial satellite launched by the Soviet Union.

1961
Yuri Gagarin

First human to travel to outer space aboard Vostok 1.

1963
Valentina Tereshkova

First woman in space, orbited Earth 48 times aboard Vostok 6.

1969
Apollo 11 — Moon Landing

Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin became the first humans to walk on the Moon.

1984
Rakesh Sharma

India's first astronaut travelled to Salyut 7 aboard Soyuz T-11.

1998
ISS Construction Begins

International Space Station assembly started with the Zarya module.

2003
Columbia Disaster

Space Shuttle Columbia broke apart on re-entry. Kalpana Chawla among the seven lost.

2012
Curiosity Rover — Mars

NASA's Curiosity rover successfully landed on Mars using the sky crane system.

2020
SpaceX Crew Dragon

First crewed commercial spaceflight — Demo-2 mission with Behnken & Hurley.

2023
Chandrayaan-3

India became the first nation to land near the Moon's south pole.
