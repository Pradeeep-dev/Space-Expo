India's Space Legends

Space Astronauts

Meet the pioneers who dared to leave Earth and reach for the stars.

01
ISRO · Cosmonaut

Rakesh Sharma

Apr 1984Launch
Salyut 7Station
7 daysDuration

India's first and only astronaut, who flew aboard Soyuz T-11 in 1984. When PM Indira Gandhi asked how India looked from space, he replied — "Saare Jahan Se Accha."

🇮🇳 Conducted yoga experiments in microgravity during his mission.
✓ Success
02
NASA · Mission Specialist

Kalpana Chawla

Nov 1997First Flight
2 MissionsFlights
30 daysIn Space

The first woman of Indian origin to travel to space. She flew on STS-87 and STS-107. Tragically lost in the Columbia disaster on February 1, 2003.

🚀 She said: "The path from dreams to success does exist."
★ Legend
03
ISRO · Director

Satish Dhawan

1972–84ISRO Chief
SLV-3First Rocket
12 yrsLeadership

Father of Indian space programme. Under his leadership, ISRO successfully launched SLV-3 and India's first remote sensing satellite IRS-1A, transforming India into a space power.

🔬 ISRO's launch centre at Sriharikota is named after him.
● Legacy
04
NASA · Commander

Sunita Williams

Dec 2006First Flight
3 MissionsFlights
322+ daysIn Space

Indian-American NASA astronaut and former US Navy officer. Has served as ISS Commander and holds records for total spacewalk time by a woman — over 50 hours outside the station.

🌍 She ran the Boston Marathon from space — on a treadmill aboard the ISS!
⬤ Active
